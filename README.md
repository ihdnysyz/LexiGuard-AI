LexiGuard-MultiAgent ⚖️
Intelligent Multi-Agent System for Cross-Border Legal Contract Review & Risk Pre-warning.

📖 Overview
LexiGuard is a sophisticated B2B enterprise solution designed to automate the review of cross-border legal documents. By leveraging a Multi-Agent Orchestration (via LangGraph), the system simulates a professional legal department workflow. It handles multi-lingual translation, identifies high-risk clauses, and generates executive summaries to streamline corporate decision-making.

Why LexiGuard?
Contextual Alignment: Goes beyond literal translation to align legal concepts across different jurisdictions (e.g., Common Law vs. Civil Law).

Risk Mitigation: Automatically flags "trap" clauses, unfair indemnity, and jurisdictional ambiguities.

Scalable Intelligence: Unlike single-prompt LLM calls, our multi-agent approach ensures specialized focus for each stage of the review.
